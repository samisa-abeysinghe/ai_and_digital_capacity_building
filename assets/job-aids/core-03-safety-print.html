DIGITRUCK JOB-AID: CORE-03

Safety, Trust & Privacy Checklist

Use this guide before you act on any digital message, call, or link. Remember the Golden Rule: Verify Before Trust (VBT).

✅ The 1-Minute Safety Check

  • Is it expected? Did I ask for this message or call?
  • Is there urgency? Is the caller trying to make me feel scared or rushed?
  • Is the source verified? Have I checked the number or the website link (.gov.lk)?
  • Is there a request for data? Am I being asked for a PIN or OTP?
❌ NEVER SHARE THESE (Regardless of who asks):
  • OTP (One Time Password)
  • ATM / Banking PIN
  • Account Passwords

🛡️ Digital Privacy Habits

  • Lock your screen: Always use a PIN or fingerprint.
  • Check Permissions: Only give apps the data they truly need.
  • Verify Links: Look closely at URLs for spelling mistakes (e.g., "bank-lk.com" instead of "bank.lk").

🚑 If you are Scammed or Hacked:

  1. Call your Bank: Immediately freeze your cards and accounts.
  2. Tell the GN Cell: Report the incident to your Grama Niladhari.
  3. Cyber Security Hotline: Call the national support desk for advice.
