Standards & Evaluation Matrix

Aligning rural digital empowerment with global benchmarks for digital literacy, safety, and AI aptitude.

Reference Frameworks

Our curriculum is evaluated against two primary global standards to ensure portability and quality:

  • UNESCO DLGF: Digital Literacy Global Framework (based on DigComp 2.1), focusing on 7 competence areas.
  • UNICEF DQ: Digital Intelligence framework, specifically for child safety, ethics, and empowerment.

Evaluation Methodology

Participants are evaluated through **Practical Observation (M1-M3)** rather than theoretical testing. A "Certified Outcome" is recorded when a participant can demonstrate the target competence Area independently on their own device.
