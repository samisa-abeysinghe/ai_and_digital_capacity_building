# AI and Digital Capacity Building

A public repository dedicated to building digital capacity and AI aptitude, providing free educational materials to the world.

## About This Project
This repository consolidates over two decades of experience (2000–2026) in technology, digital transformation, and capacity building. The goal is to democratize access to high-quality digital literacy and AI training materials, empowering individuals and communities—especially in rural and underserved areas—to thrive in the digital age.

## Key Areas
- **AI Aptitude & Awareness:** Practical guides on understanding and utilizing AI responsibly.
- **Digital Capacity Building:** Curriculum and frameworks for various personas (Youth, Women, Entrepreneurs, etc.).
- **DigiTruck Rural Empowerment:** Specialized materials for mobile digital classroom initiatives.

## Contents
- `/digitruck`: Curriculum and context for the DigiTruck rural empowerment program.
- `/ai`: (Coming Soon) AI-specific training modules and prompting guides.
- `/capacity-building`: (Coming Soon) General digital literacy frameworks.
