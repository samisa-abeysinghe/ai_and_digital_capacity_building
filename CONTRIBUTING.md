# Contributing to AI and Digital Capacity Building

First off, thank you for considering contributing! It's people like you who will help build digital capacity worldwide.

## How Can I Contribute?

### Translating Content
Our goal is to have all materials available in **English, Sinhala, and Tamil**. If you are fluent in these languages, your help with translations is invaluable.
- Translations should be placed in the respective `content/[lang]/` directories.

### Adding New Modules
If you have expertise in AI or Digital Literacy:
1. Check the existing modules in `content/en/`.
2. Propose a new module by opening an Issue first.
3. Follow the established metadata and maturity model structure.

### Improving the Website
We aim for a "phone-first," low-bandwidth, and accessible site. Improvements to CSS/JS that enhance performance or accessibility are welcome.

## Style Guide
- **Tone:** Mentoring, empowering, and clear.
- **Accessibility:** Use semantic HTML. Ensure high contrast.
- **AI Ethics:** Always emphasize responsible and verified use of AI.
