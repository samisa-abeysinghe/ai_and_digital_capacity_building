## 0) What this pack is
This markdown consolidates the project requirements, information architecture, and content design rules for building a **static-site curriculum library** for Sri Lanka’s **DigiTruck** rural digital empowerment program.

---

## 1) Delivery model & governance assumptions
### 1.1 Administrative delivery ladder
Delivery follows the administrative structure:
**National → Province → District → DS (Divisional Secretariat) → GN (Grama Niladhari Division)**

### 1.2 Operational Flow: The "Week Map"
DigiTruck operates on a cyclical "Week Map" for each GN Division:
- **Days 1-2 (Preparation):** GN Cell mobilization; selection of "dedicated crowd" for first rounds.
- **Day 3 (DigiTruck Day):** High-impact immersion session.
- **Days 4-7 (Continuity):** Local Trainer Pod support; field practice for ATM/QR; Ministerial service registration follow-ups.

### 1.3 GN Digital Empowerment Cell (GDEC)
GN-level continuity is enabled by a GN Digital Empowerment Cell:
- Grama Niladhari (Convenor)
- Development Officer
- Women & Children Officer
- Health Officer / PHI
- Youth Officer

## 2) Personas & Pilot Focus
Primary personas for the Pilot Phase:
1. **GN Cell Officials** (Sustainability anchors)
2. **Women** (Community pillars)
3. **Children** (Future-ready generation)
4. **Youth**
5. **Rural Entrepreneurs** (Livelihood impact)
6. **Elderly / Digitally Excluded Adults**

---

## 3) Core spine (High-Utility Content)
All modules must align with **UNESCO Digital Literacy Standards** and prioritize these use cases:
1. **Digital Confidence & Fundamentals** (Mature yet simple visuals)
2. **Communication & Digital Etiquette**
3. **Safety, Trust & Privacy** (Security basics; "Verify Before Trust")
4. **Government Digital Services** (Ministerial use cases: e.g., Pensions, Health, DMT)
5. **QR Code Payments** (LankaQR integration; merchant vs consumer flows)
6. **Digital Banking & ATM Use** (Physical-to-Digital transitions; ticketing)
7. **AI Awareness** (What AI is/isn’t; ethics; limitations)
8. **AI Aptitude** (Prompting; "Verify Everything" rule)
9. **Productivity Habits** (Tools to finish work faster)
10. **Pathways to Opportunity**
11. **Community Continuity** (GN anchored support)

---

## 4) DigiTruck day structure (7 sessions)
- Session 1 (45m): Digital Confidence & Setup  
- Session 2 (45m): Communication & Etiquette  
- Session 3 (45m): Safety, Trust & Privacy  
- Session 4 (60–75m): Gov e-Services & Ministerial Portals  
- Session 5 (60–75m): QR Payments & ATM/Banking Lab  
- Session 6 (60–75m): AI Awareness + AI Aptitude Lab  
- Session 7 (60–75m): Capstone + GN Handoff

---

## 5) Curriculum maturity model
- **M0 Awareness:** knows it exists + basic risks
- **M1 Basic Use:** can perform with guidance
- **M2 Independent Use:** can complete tasks safely alone
- **M3 Applied Outcomes:** applies to life/livelihood; may help others

---

## 8) Non-negotiables (Quality & Safety)
- **Mature yet Easy:** Content must be easy to understand but visually professional (no "childish" design).
- **Trust-by-design:** explicit fraud prevention; never share OTP/PIN/password.
- **Verify Everything:** AI is a helper, not a source of absolute truth.
- **Low-bandwidth & phone-first:** pages must render fast, minimal JS.
- **Bilingual readiness:** Sinhala/Tamil/English supported.
