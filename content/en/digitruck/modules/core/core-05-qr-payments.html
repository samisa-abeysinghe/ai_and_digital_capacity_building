CORE-05: QR Code Payments (LankaQR)

Master the fastest way to pay and get paid. A practical guide to using QR codes safely for daily transactions and micro-business.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M2 Independent Use UNESCO Alignment: Digital Content (3.1) Session: S5 (60-75m)

1. Concept: What is LankaQR?

LankaQR is the national standard for QR payments in Sri Lanka. It allows you to pay at any merchant using any banking or payment app on your phone. No cash, no cards, just a simple scan.

  • Interoperability: One QR works for all apps.
  • Instant Settlement: Money moves immediately between accounts.
  • Low Cost: Often cheaper or free compared to card payments for small merchants.

🛠 Lab Activity: Your First Scan (Consumer Flow)

Using the DigiTruck dummy payment app, practice the 4-step scan process:

  • Step 1: Open your banking/payment app and select "Scan."
  • Step 2: Point the camera at the LankaQR code on the counter.
  • Step 3: Enter the exact amount and check the merchant name on the screen.
  • Step 4: Confirm with your PIN/Biometrics and show the "Success" screen to the merchant.

⚠️ Safety Check: Verify the Merchant

Before you confirm the payment, always check the **Merchant Name** displayed in your app. Ensure it matches the shop you are standing in. If a merchant asks you to scan a QR sent via a private message (WhatsApp/Viber), be extremely cautious.

2. For Entrepreneurs: Receiving Payments

If you sell goods or services, having your own LankaQR code means you never lose a sale because of "no change."

  • Dynamic QR: You generate a code for a specific amount.
  • Static QR: You print one code that stays on your counter; the customer enters the amount.
  • Record Keeping: Every sale is automatically logged in your app history.

📄 Job Aid: QR Payment Etiquette

  1. Always wait for the "Payment Successful" sound or screen before leaving.
  2. Keep your phone screen clean for better scanning.
  3. Never scan a QR code if the physical sticker looks tampered with or "pasted over."
  4. Verify the amount twice before hitting "Pay."
