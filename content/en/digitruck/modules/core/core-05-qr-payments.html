CORE-05: QR Code Payments (LankaQR)

Master the fastest way to pay and get paid. A practical guide to using LankaQR safely for daily transactions and micro-business growth.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes UNESCO Alignment: Digital Content (3.1) Session: S5 (60-75m)

1. Concept: The LankaQR Standard

In Sri Lanka, you don't need multiple apps for different shops. Any banking app (e.g., ComBank Q+, Flash, Genie, Frimi, or Sampath Vishwa) can scan the LankaQR code found at local merchants.

  • Instant Settlement: Unlike cards, the money reaches the merchant immediately.
  • No Change Needed: Perfect for small items (bus tickets, vegetables, tea).
  • Transaction Proof: Every payment creates a permanent digital receipt in your app.

🛠 Lab Activity: The 4-Step Professional Scan

Using the DigiTruck dummy payment app, practice the "Merchant Verification" flow:

  • Step 1: Open your app and select "Scan LankaQR."
  • Step 2: Point the camera at the merchant's code.
  • Step 3: Stop & Verify: Does the name on the screen match the name of the shop?
  • Step 4: Enter the amount, confirm, and show the "Success" screen to the merchant.

⚠️ Local Scam Alert: Fake Receipt Screenshots

If you are a merchant, never trust a customer just showing you a screenshot of a "Success" screen. Always wait for the SMS Alert on your own phone or check your Transaction History to confirm the money has actually arrived.

2. For Entrepreneurs: Your Digital Cashier

Accepting digital payments builds a "Credit History" that helps you get bank loans later.

  • Static QR: Your permanent code on the counter (customer enters amount).
  • Dynamic QR: You generate a specific code for one sale (no typing errors).
  • Daily Reconciliation: Use your app to see your total sales at the end of the day.

🏆 M3 Livelihood Challenge

Task: Set up a "Mock Sale" for your specific business (e.g., selling a bottle of honey for 1,200 LKR). Generate a Dynamic QR for this amount and have a "Digital Buddy" scan it to verify the merchant name is correct.

🤝 The "Help-a-Neighbor" Protocol

To reach M3 Mastery, you must help others. Your task:

"Identify one person in your village who is still afraid of digital payments. Show them how to check their transaction history so they can see their money is safe."
