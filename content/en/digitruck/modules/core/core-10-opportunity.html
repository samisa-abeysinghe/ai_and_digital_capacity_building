CORE-10: Pathways to Opportunity

Your bridge to the future. Learn how to find verified platforms for learning, employment, and digital certifications.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M2-M3 Independent/Applied UNESCO Alignment: Digital Literacy (6.1) Session: S7 (45m)

1. Concept: The Global Classroom

Digital literacy is the first step toward lifelong learning. The internet is a vast library, but you must know how to find the "Official" and "Verified" shelves.

  • Verified Learning: Platforms like Coursera, edX, or local government vocational portals.
  • Digital Certifications: Why a "Verified Badge" matters more than just "watching a video."
  • The "Free vs Paid" Balance: Finding high-quality free resources without falling for scams.

🛠 Lab Activity: Finding Your Learning Path

Use the DigiTruck Opportunity Simulator to explore these three paths:

  • Path 1: The Learner. Find a free 1-hour course on "Basic AI for Business" and check the certificate requirements.
  • Path 2: The Job Seeker. Navigate a verified gig-work or local job portal and identify the required "Digital Skills" for a role.
  • Path 3: The Entrepreneur. Find a marketplace or export directory to see how city-based or global sellers list their products.

🛑 Avoid "Get Rich Quick" Scams

Many sites promise "Easy Money Online" or "Guaranteed Jobs." Remember:

  • Never pay an upfront fee to get a job.
  • Be skeptical of "Automated Trading" or "Crypto" promises.
  • Trust official platforms that have clear reviews and government recognition.

2. Building a Digital Portfolio

Your "Digital Resume" is how the world sees your skills. Start building it today.

  • Documentation: Keeping photos of your work, certificates, and positive feedback.
  • Professional Profile: Creating a simple, professional page or social media profile dedicated to your work.
  • Networking: Joining verified local groups of peers and mentors (like the GN Local Trainer Pod).

📄 Job Aid: 3 Steps to Your Next Opportunity

  1. Search: Use keywords like "Free [Skill] Certification" or "Verified [Industry] Marketplace."
  2. Verify: Check if the platform is recognized by UNESCO, the Government, or major industry leaders.
  3. Act: Set a goal to complete one small learning module per week to keep your skills fresh.
