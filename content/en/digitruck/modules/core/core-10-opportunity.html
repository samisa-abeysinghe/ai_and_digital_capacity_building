CORE-10: Pathways to Opportunity

Your bridge to the future. Learn how to find verified platforms for learning, employment, and digital certifications within the Sri Lankan and global economy.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes UNESCO Alignment: Digital Literacy (6.1) Session: S7 (45m)

1. Concept: The Local-to-Global Opportunity Map

Digital literacy in Sri Lanka opens doors to both local employment and global freelancing. The key is knowing which platforms are "Official" and "Verified."

  • Vocational Training: Navigating TVEC (Tertiary and Vocational Education Commission) portals and local vocational training apps.
  • Marketplaces: Using Daraz, Kapruka, or local Facebook Business groups to reach city-based customers.
  • Employment Portals: Identifying verified jobs on TopJobs.lk, Ikman.lk, or government vacancy gazettes.

🛠 Lab Activity: Finding Your Path

Explore these three M3 opportunity paths:

  • Path 1: The Learner. Find one free digital course on the **ICTA** or **DP Education** platform and bookmark the "Start" button.
  • Path 2: The Seller. Search for your specific product (e.g., "Handmade baskets") on a major marketplace and note the "Seller Registration" requirements.
  • Path 3: The Job Seeker. Identify a "Remote Work" category on a verified portal and list 2 digital skills you need to master next.

🛑 Opportunity Scam Alert

Many "Work from Home" ads on social media are scams. **Never** pay an "Admin Fee" or "Deposit" to a personal bank account to get a job. If an offer sounds too good to be true, ask your **GN Cell** to verify the company's legitimacy.

🏆 M3 Livelihood Challenge

Task: Create a "Digital Skills Roadmap." Write a 3-month goal (e.g., "I will complete a basic bookkeeping course") and identify the exact **Verified Platform** where you will take it. Share this goal with your "Digital Buddy" for accountability.

🤝 The "Help-a-Neighbor" Protocol

M3 Mastery means uplifting your community. Your task:

"Next time you see a talented youth or entrepreneur in your village, show them the official TVEC or ICTA learning portals. Help them see that their phone is a door to a new career."
