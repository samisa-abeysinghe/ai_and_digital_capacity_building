CORE-09: Problem-Solving & Productivity Habits

Work smarter, not harder. Master the digital tools and habits that help you organize your life, save time, and solve problems independently.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M2 Independent Use UNESCO Alignment: Problem Solving (5.1/5.4) Session: S7 (45m)

1. Concept: Digital Organization

Productivity is about having a system. Instead of remembering everything, we use digital tools to hold our plans, documents, and reminders.

  • Search vs Browse: Learn how to find any file or email in seconds using keywords.
  • Cloud Storage: Keeping your important documents (NIC, Certificates) safe even if you lose your phone.
  • Task Management: Moving from "mental notes" to digital checklists.

🛠 Lab Activity: Setting Up Your Personal Workflow

Practice these three essential productivity habits:

  • Habit 1: The Calendar. Add an appointment for "GN Follow-up" next Tuesday and set a 1-hour reminder.
  • Habit 2: The Checklist. Create a "Market Day Prep" list with 5 items. Practice ticking them off.
  • Habit 3: The Search. Use the phone's search bar to find a specific photo or contact using only a partial name.

⚠️ Backup Security

While "Cloud Storage" (Google Drive, iCloud) is useful, it must be protected with a strong password and Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). If you lose your password and your recovery phone number, you may lose your documents forever.

2. Independent Problem Solving

A productive person knows how to find answers when they are stuck.

  • The "Help" Menu: Every app has a hidden guide.
  • Search as a Tutor: Using "How to..." searches effectively.
  • Community Support: Knowing how to use the GN Cell or local trainer pod for help.

📄 Job Aid: Daily Digital Reset

Every evening, take 2 minutes to:

  1. Clear your notification bar (reduce stress).
  2. Check your calendar for tomorrow.
  3. Ensure your device is charging for the next day.
  4. Close apps you are no longer using to save battery.
