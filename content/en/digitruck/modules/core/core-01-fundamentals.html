CORE-01: Digital Confidence & Fundamentals

Master your workstation for life and livelihood. Learn to manage your device, data, and digital identity with professional confidence.

Maturity Entry: M0 Awareness Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes UNESCO Alignment: Hardware/Software (2.1) Session: S1 (45m)

1. Concept: Connectivity & Input Mastery

In Sri Lanka, digital access depends on managing your mobile data and typing efficiently in your preferred language. We treat the smartphone as a portable office.

  • Data Optimization: Understanding 4G/5G signal indicators and data-saving settings.
  • Language Input: Setting up the Helakuru or Google Indic keyboard for Sinhala/Tamil typing.
  • Device Health: Managing storage to prevent "system lag" during official tasks.

🛠 Lab Activity: The Professional Workstation Check

Perform these essential configurations for a professional workflow:

  • Step 1: Identify your device's exact **Data Usage** menu.
  • Step 2: Install and switch to a Sinhala/Tamil keyboard (e.g., Helakuru).
  • Step 3: Use the phone's **Search Bar** to locate a specific setting without scrolling.
  • Step 4: Check your **Signal Strength** in different parts of the DigiTruck.

⚠️ Data Warning: Automatic Background Updates

Many apps "eat" your data in the background. Learn to turn off "Auto-Updates" over Mobile Data to ensure you have balance remaining for essential government and banking services.

🏆 M3 Livelihood Challenge

Task: Create a "Professional Bio" note on your phone (using a notes app) in your native language. This must include your Name, Contact Number, and a 1-sentence description of your skill or business. Save this as a "Digital Identity card" for quick sharing.

🤝 The "Help-a-Neighbor" Protocol

M3 Mastery means empowering your community. Your task:

"Next time a friend says their phone is 'slow,' show them how to clear their app cache and check which apps are using the most background data."
