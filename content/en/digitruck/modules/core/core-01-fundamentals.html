CORE-01: Digital Confidence & Fundamentals

Build a professional foundation. Understand your device, your connection, and your digital presence.

Maturity Entry: M0 Awareness Maturity Target: M1 Basic Use UNESCO Alignment: Hardware/Software (2.1) Session: S1 (45m)

1. Concept: The Anatomy of Digital Use

Confidence starts with understanding the tools. We treat the smartphone not as a "toy," but as a portable workstation for life and livelihood.

  • Hardware Mastery: Buttons, ports, and battery health.
  • Connectivity: Understanding the difference between Mobile Data (4G/5G) and Wi-Fi (Private vs Public).
  • Operating Systems: Navigating menus and settings with ease.

🛠 Lab Activity: The 5-Point Device Check

Perform these essential checks on your device:

  • Step 1: Check signal strength and data usage limits.
  • Step 2: Identify "Permissions" for your most-used apps.
  • Step 3: Configure professional display settings (Text size, Brightness).
  • Step 4: Check available storage space.
  • Step 5: Locate the "Emergency" or "Identity" section in settings.

⚠️ Data Protection: Public Wi-Fi

Avoid logging into bank accounts or government portals while on "Free Public Wi-Fi." Use your own mobile data for sensitive transactions to ensure a private "tunnel" for your information.

2. Digital Presence & Identity

In the digital world, your NIC and phone number are your primary keys. Learn how to protect them.

  • Digital NIC: How to store and present your identity securely.
  • The SIM Lock: An extra layer of protection for your phone number.
  • Email vs Phone Number: Choosing the right "anchor" for your accounts.

📄 Job Aid: Fundamentals Checklist

  1. Keep your device software updated (Security Patches).
  2. Use a screen lock (PIN, Pattern, or Biometrics).
  3. Know your "Data Balance" before starting heavy tasks.
  4. Keep a backup of your important contacts and photos.
