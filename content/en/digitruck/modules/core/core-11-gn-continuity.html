CORE-11: GN Continuity & Support System

You are not alone. Learn how to stay connected to your local **GN Digital Empowerment Cell (GDEC)** and Trainer Pod for ongoing support after the DigiTruck leaves.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes Focus: Community Sustainability Session: S7 (Handoff)

1. Concept: The Village Support Engine

The DigiTruck visit is the "Spark," but your sustainable growth is managed by the **GN Digital Empowerment Cell (GDEC)**. This official body ensures that no citizen is left behind in the digital transition.

  • The GN Officer: Your primary point for verifying government service links and safety.
  • The Local Trainer Pod: Peer mentors who live in your community and provide hands-on help.
  • The Digital Clinic: Regular 60-minute "Micro-Sessions" held in your GN office to solve specific problems.

🛠 Lab Activity: Setting Up Your Support Keys

Before you leave, ensure your "Continuity" is configured:

  • Task 1: Save the verified WhatsApp contact for your **GN Helpdesk** in your phone.
  • Task 2: Identify your assigned **Local Trainer** and introduce yourself.
  • Task 3: Note the date of the next **GN Micro-Session** in your digital calendar.

🛡 The "Official Verification" Rule

If you receive a strange message about a "Digital Fine" or a "Pension Error," **do not act.** Take your phone to your GN office. They have the official tools to verify if the request is real. **Trust your village officials first.**

🏆 M3 Livelihood Challenge

Task: Commit to becoming a **Digital Support Buddy**. Identify one person in your immediate neighborhood who could not attend the DigiTruck. Commit to showing them **one skill** you learned today (e.g., scanning a QR or checking a balance) during the next 7 days. Record this commitment with your GN official.

🤝 The Sustainability Protocol

M3 Mastery means moving from a "Student" to a "Mentor." Your task:

"Participation in the GDEC community groups is not just for help—it is for sharing your success stories. When you successfully use a digital tool to save time or money, share it! You are the champion of your division."
