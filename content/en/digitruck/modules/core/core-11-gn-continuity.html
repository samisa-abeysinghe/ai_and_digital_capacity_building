CORE-11: GN Continuity & Support System

You are not alone. Learn how to stay connected to your local GN Cell and Trainer Pod for ongoing support after the DigiTruck leaves.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes Focus: Community Sustainability Session: S7 (Handoff)

1. Concept: The GN Digital Empowerment Cell (GDEC)

The DigiTruck is your "Day 3" immersion, but your "Day 4 and Beyond" is supported by your local GN Cell. They are the sustainable heart of digital growth in your division.

  • Convenor: Your Grama Niladhari (GN).
  • The Officers: Development, Women & Children, Health, and Youth officers.
  • The Local Trainer Pod: Peer mentors who live in your community and can offer quick help.

🛠 Lab Activity: Setting Up Your Support Network

Ensure you leave the DigiTruck with your "Continuity Keys" ready:

  • Step 1: Save the verified WhatsApp group or contact number for your GN's Digital Helpdesk.
  • Step 2: Identify your nearest "Local Trainer" and save their contact info for peer-support.
  • Step 3: Mark the date for the next "GN Micro-Session" or "Digital Clinic" in your calendar.

🛡 Community Trust

Your GN Cell is an official body. If you receive a suspicious message or feel scared about a digital transaction, always go to your GN first. They can verify if a government request is real or a scam.

2. Becoming a Digital Mentor

Maturity M3 means "Applied Outcomes," which includes helping others. As you become more confident, you can join the Local Trainer Pod.

  • Share Knowledge: Help your neighbor scan a QR code or check their pension status.
  • Identify Gaps: Tell your GN officer about specific digital challenges your community is facing.
  • Grow Together: Participate in GN micro-sessions to keep your skills updated.

📄 Job Aid: Your Continuity Checklist

  1. Do I have my GN Cell's digital contact information?
  2. Have I registered for the next follow-up clinic?
  3. Do I have my "Safety Cheat Sheet" printed or saved?
  4. Who is my "Digital Buddy" for peer-to-peer help?
