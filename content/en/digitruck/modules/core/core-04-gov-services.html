CORE-04: Government e-Services & Ministerial Portals

Save time and avoid queues. Learn how to interact directly with official government services for pensions, health, and documentation.

Maturity Entry: M1 Basic Use Maturity Target: M2 Independent Use UNESCO Alignment: Information Literacy (1.1) Session: S4 (60-75m)

1. Concept: The "No-Queue" Government

Most government interactions—applying for a permit, checking a pension, or booking an appointment—can now be started or completed from your phone. This is about efficiency and direct access.

  • Official Source Verification: Ensuring you are on a ".gov.lk" site.
  • Digital Identity: Using your NIC and phone number to verify who you are.
  • Form Uploads: Turning physical documents into clear digital photos.

🛠 Lab Activity: High-Utility Use Cases

Navigate the official ministerial simulators for these three core services:

  • Pensions & Social Benefits: Learn how to check status and payment dates without visiting the DS office.
  • Health Services: Practice booking a clinic appointment or checking lab results online.
  • Motor Traffic (DMT): Learn how to check for revenue license status and fines.

🛑 Beware of "Middleman" Sites

Always check the web address (URL). Scammers often create sites that look like government portals but end in ".net" or ".com" instead of **.gov.lk**. Never pay a "convenience fee" to a third-party website for a free government service.

2. Digital Document Handling

A "Digital Copy" is only useful if it is readable. Learn the professional way to digitize your paperwork.

  • Lighting & Focus: Take photos in bright light; ensure all four corners of the document are visible.
  • File Size: Learn how to compress a photo so it fits government upload limits.
  • Secure Storage: Keep a "Digital Folder" on your phone for important certificates.

📄 Job Aid: 3 Steps to Any Gov Service

  1. Search: Use the official government portal directory (e.g., gic.gov.lk).
  2. Login: Use your verified mobile number or email.
  3. Receipt: Always take a screenshot of your "Reference Number" or "Submission Receipt" immediately.
