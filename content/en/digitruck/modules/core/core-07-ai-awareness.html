CORE-07: AI Awareness

Demystifying Artificial Intelligence. Understand what AI is, its local impact in Sri Lanka, and its role as a "Digital Helper."

Maturity Entry: M0 Awareness Maturity Target: M3 Applied Outcomes UNESCO Alignment: Information Literacy (1.3) Session: S6 (45m)

1. Concept: AI in the Sri Lankan Context

AI is already helping us every day. From voice-to-text in Sinhala/Tamil to predicting weather for farmers, it is a tool for efficiency, not a replacement for humans.

  • Language AI: How your phone "guesses" the next word in a Sinhala/Tamil message.
  • Visual AI: Using your camera to identify a pest on a plant or translate a English sign into your native language.
  • The Helper Model: Treat AI like a smart intern who knows a lot but still needs careful supervision.

🛡 The "Local Hallucination" Warning

AI can be confidently wrong, especially about local laws, village-specific data, or exact historical dates in Sri Lanka. **Never** trust AI for legal or medical advice without verifying with an official government portal (.gov.lk) or a professional.

🛠 Lab Activity: Seeing AI at Work

Identify where AI is helping your community today:

  • Task 1: Use a translation app to translate a professional English sentence into clear Sinhala/Tamil.
  • Task 2: Use "Voice Search" in your native language to find the "Current price of Fertilizer" or "Next holiday in Sri Lanka."
  • Task 3: Use your phone's camera to "Scan and Search" for an object inside the DigiTruck.

🏆 M3 Livelihood Challenge

Task: Use AI to generate a list of "3 Best Practices for Selling Organic Produce in Cities." You must then **Verify** at least one of these practices by searching for a real success story from a Sri Lankan agriculture website or news portal.

🤝 The "Help-a-Neighbor" Protocol

M3 Mastery means reducing digital fear. Your task:

"Show a neighbor how to use the 'Voice Input' (Microphone) button to send a long WhatsApp message in their own language. Explain that the AI is 'listening' to help them type faster."
