Children's (Grades 6-13) Track

Building the future-ready generation. Moving beyond digital "play" to digital "aptitude" and responsible use.

Target Outcomes

  • Digital Ethics: Understand the impact of your digital footprint and the "Verify Everything" rule.
  • Future-Proof Skills: Build strong fundamentals in hardware, software, and problem-solving.
