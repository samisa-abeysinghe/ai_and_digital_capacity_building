Operational Week Map

From Mobilization to Continuity. The 7-day cycle that ensures digital skills take root in the GN division.

Phase 1: Mobilization & Prep (Days 1-2)

The GN Digital Empowerment Cell (GDEC) prepares the community.

  • Crowd Selection: Identify 20-30 "dedicated crowd" participants per session.
  • Venue Check: Ensure safe parking and power access for the DigiTruck.
  • Pre-Truck Clinic: Local trainers help participants check their devices and data balances.

Phase 2: The DigiTruck Day (Day 3)

High-intensity immersive learning inside the mobile classroom.

Morning Sessions

  • S1: Fundamentals
  • S2: Communication
  • S3: Safety & Trust (The Gate)

Afternoon Sessions

  • S4: Gov e-Services Lab
  • S5: QR & Banking Lab
  • S6: AI Awareness & Aptitude
  • S7: GN Handoff

Phase 3: Continuity & Handoff (Days 4-7)

Ensuring the skills are applied in the real world.

  • Field Practice: Local trainers accompany participants to the local shop for a real QR payment.
  • Registration Support: GDEC officials help finalize ministerial portal registrations.
  • Peer Groups: Formation of "Digital Support Pods" among youth and women for ongoing help.

💡 The Continuity Rule

The DigiTruck is the spark, but the GN Cell is the flame. Skills only become "M3 Mastery" when used repeatedly in the division after the truck has moved to the next location.
