Daily Schedule: Schoolchildren

Focus: Learning productivity, safe internet habits, and AI study buddies.

Viewing as: Learner
09:00

S1: Study Folders & Setup

Organizing device for schoolwork and mastering search skills.

Module CORE-01
Check that children know how to create a folder specifically for "Science" or "History."
09:45

S2: Respectful Online Behavior

WhatsApp rules, clear writing, and spotting misinformation.

Module CORE-02
Group discussion: "What do you do if someone is mean in a group chat?"
10:30

S3: Cyberbullying & Privacy

The "Hard Gate": Passwords, privacy settings, and reporting to adults.

Module CORE-03
Focus on the "Ask-an-adult" rule for suspicious links.
11:30

S4: Official Knowledge Lab

Finding official study portals and safe document uploads.

Module CORE-04
Demo the National Library or Education Ministry portals.
13:30

S5: Money Safety & Scam Links

Recognizing fake game offers and never sharing family OTPs.

Module CORE-05
Teach them to identify "Too good to be true" digital prizes.
14:45

S6: AI Study Buddy Lab

Safe prompting for research, but verifying every answer.

Module CORE-08
Activity: "Ask AI for a fact, then find a book or site that proves it."
16:00

S7: Study Plan & Assessment

Personal learning routine and next clinic date.

Module CORE-11
Ensure they know their "Digital Buddy" in class.
