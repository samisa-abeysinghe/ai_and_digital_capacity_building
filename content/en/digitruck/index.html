DigiTruck Rural Curriculum

A 7-day operational "Week Map" designed to move rural communities from digital awareness to independent use. Supported by the GN Digital Empowerment Cell.

The "Week Map" Cycle

Days 1-2: Prep

Mobilization and selection of dedicated crowds (Officials, Women, Children).

Day 3: Truck Day

High-impact immersion session with specialized equipment.

Days 4-7: Continuity

Local Trainer Pod support and field registration follow-ups.
